# Castle Dash - 2D Platformer Game

Castle Dash is an exciting 2D platformer game developed using Python and the Pygame library. In this fast-paced adventure, players take on the role of a fearless character navigating a treacherous castle filled with obstacles, challenges, and surprises.

## Key Features

- **Engaging Gameplay:** Enjoy responsive controls, smooth animations, and a dynamic gaming experience.
- **Random Obstacles:** Test your reflexes by dodging falling swords and other hazards that appear randomly.
- **Power-Up Bonanza:** Collect power-ups to enhance your character's abilities and increase your chances of survival.
- **Pause and Resume:** Take a break or fine-tune settings with the in-game pause menu, allowing you to continue your quest at your own pace.
- **High Score Challenge:** Compete against yourself and others by striving for the highest score, based on your survival duration.
- **Interactive Menus:** Navigate through user-friendly menus for an enjoyable gaming experience.
- **Immersive Sound:** Immerse yourself in the game's world with background music and sound effects that enhance the gaming atmosphere.

These instructions will help you get a copy of the project up and running on your local machine for development and testing purposes.
